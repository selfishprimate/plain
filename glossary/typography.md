# Typography

Font combinations and typography systems that establish readability, hierarchy, and personality in your interface. Each combination balances aesthetics with performance and accessibility.

Choose a typography system that complements your design style and serves your content needs:

### Classic Serif
Timeless elegance with traditional serif fonts that convey authority and sophistication.
- **Heading:** Playfair Display, Merriweather, Georgia
- **Body:** Lora, Crimson Text, Georgia
- **Best for:** Editorial sites, luxury brands, academic platforms
- **Characteristics:** High readability, traditional feel, strong personality

### Modern Sans
Clean and contemporary with geometric sans-serif fonts that feel fresh and minimal.
- **Heading:** Inter, Helvetica Neue, DM Sans
- **Body:** Inter, System UI, -apple-system
- **Best for:** Tech products, SaaS platforms, modern apps
- **Characteristics:** Excellent screen rendering, neutral personality, highly legible

### Humanist Sans
Friendly and approachable with organic letterforms that feel warm and inviting.
- **Heading:** Open Sans, Lato, Source Sans Pro
- **Body:** Open Sans, Lato, Roboto
- **Best for:** Community platforms, educational tools, healthcare apps
- **Characteristics:** Friendly appearance, good readability, versatile usage

### Tech Mono
Developer-focused with monospace fonts that create a technical, precise atmosphere.
- **Heading:** JetBrains Mono, Fira Code, IBM Plex Mono
- **Body:** Inter, System UI, Roboto
- **Best for:** Developer tools, code editors, technical documentation
- **Characteristics:** Code-friendly, technical aesthetic, clear differentiation

### Editorial Mix
Sophisticated pairing of serif headers with sans-serif body for optimal reading experience.
- **Heading:** Libre Baskerville, Playfair Display, Noto Serif
- **Body:** Source Sans Pro, Open Sans, Inter
- **Best for:** Blogs, magazines, news sites, content platforms
- **Characteristics:** Strong hierarchy, excellent readability, professional appearance

### Playful Display
Fun and expressive with display fonts that add personality and energy.
- **Heading:** Fredoka, Comfortaa, Quicksand
- **Body:** Open Sans, Nunito, Rubik
- **Best for:** Children's apps, games, creative tools
- **Characteristics:** Rounded edges, friendly feel, high impact

### Geometric Sans
Precise and mathematical with perfectly geometric letterforms.
- **Heading:** Montserrat, Poppins, Futura
- **Body:** Montserrat, Poppins, Avenir
- **Best for:** Design tools, architecture sites, modern brands
- **Characteristics:** Clean geometry, modern aesthetic, strong personality

### Swiss Style
Minimal and functional following Swiss typography principles.
- **Heading:** Helvetica, Arial, Univers
- **Body:** Helvetica, Arial, System UI
- **Best for:** Minimal interfaces, documentation, corporate sites
- **Characteristics:** Ultimate simplicity, high legibility, timeless design

### Brutalist Type
Bold and unconventional with strong typographic statements.
- **Heading:** Space Grotesk, Archivo Black, Oswald
- **Body:** Space Mono, Roboto Mono, Inter
- **Best for:** Creative agencies, experimental projects, bold brands
- **Characteristics:** High contrast, strong presence, unconventional pairings

### Elegant Script
Refined combinations featuring script or decorative elements for special emphasis.
- **Heading:** Cormorant Garamond, Abril Fatface, Didot
- **Body:** Montserrat, Raleway, Open Sans
- **Best for:** Fashion brands, wedding sites, luxury products
- **Characteristics:** High contrast, elegant flourishes, sophisticated feel

### Accessible First
Optimized for maximum readability and accessibility across all users.
- **Heading:** Atkinson Hyperlegible, Lexend, Clear Sans
- **Body:** Atkinson Hyperlegible, Lexend, Open Dyslexic
- **Best for:** Accessibility-focused apps, educational platforms, government sites
- **Characteristics:** Enhanced legibility, clear letterforms, inclusive design

### Variable Weight
Single font families with extensive weight ranges for ultimate flexibility.
- **Heading:** Inter (100-900), Roboto Flex, Source Sans Variable
- **Body:** Same as heading with different weights
- **Best for:** Design systems, scalable products, responsive designs
- **Characteristics:** Consistent design, flexible hierarchy, performance benefits

### Retro Revival
Nostalgic combinations that evoke specific time periods while staying functional.
- **Heading:** Bebas Neue, Alfa Slab One, Righteous
- **Body:** Roboto, Open Sans, Lato
- **Best for:** Retro brands, vintage marketplaces, nostalgic products
- **Characteristics:** Period-appropriate style, strong character, memorable impact

### Corporate Professional
Conservative and reliable combinations that build trust and credibility.
- **Heading:** IBM Plex Sans, Roboto, Arial
- **Body:** IBM Plex Sans, Roboto, System UI
- **Best for:** Enterprise software, financial services, B2B platforms
- **Characteristics:** Professional appearance, excellent legibility, cross-platform consistency

### Artistic Expression
Creative combinations that prioritize personality and visual impact.
- **Heading:** Abril Fatface, Playfair Display, Bodoni
- **Body:** Karla, Work Sans, Nunito Sans
- **Best for:** Creative portfolios, art platforms, design showcases
- **Characteristics:** Strong contrast, visual interest, creative freedom

---

*Typography is the voice of your interface. Choose fonts that not only look good but also support your content strategy, load quickly, and remain readable across all devices and contexts.*
