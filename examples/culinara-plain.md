**Project Name:** Culinara

**Idea Statement:** Culinara is a smart recipe discovery platform that helps home cooks reduce food waste and simplify meal planning by suggesting delicious recipes based on ingredients already in their pantry, fridge, and freezer.

**Core Purpose:** Solve the everyday problem of "what should I cook?" by transforming ingredient inventory into personalized meal suggestions, making cooking more accessible, sustainable, and stress-free for busy individuals and families.

# Functional Requirements

## Must Have

- **Ingredient Inventory Management**: Users can add, remove, and quantity ingredients they have available. Supports manual entry and category organization (Produce, Proteins, Grains, etc.).
- **Smart Recipe Suggestions**: Algorithm-powered engine that suggests recipes matching available ingredients. Displays recipes requiring 80%+ ingredient match prominently, with options to see recipes requiring minor additional items.
- **Recipe Details Page**: Full recipe view including ingredients, instructions, prep/cook time, servings, difficulty level, dietary tags (vegan, gluten-free, etc.), and user ratings.

- **Shopping List Generation**: Auto-generate shopping lists from selected recipes, deduplicating ingredients with available inventory.
- **Nutritional Information**: Display calories, protein, carbs, fats, and fiber for each recipe per serving.
- **Recipe Ratings and Reviews**: Community ratings and user-submitted reviews with photos (text and images optional).
- **Dietary Preference Profiles**: Users set dietary preferences (vegan, keto, paleo, etc.) which auto-filter results without manual filter selection each time.
